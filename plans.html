Common Inquiries

Is there a minimum commitment?

We request a 3-month initial commitment to ensure we can deliver substantial value and integrate fully with your team's workflow.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes. We understand business fluctuates. You can pause your subscription for up to 3 months once every calendar year.

What constitutes a "request"?

A request is any single task that can be defined in a brief. For example: "Design a landing page" or "Implement a CMS feature".

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the high-quality nature of our work, we do not offer refunds. However, we iterate on designs until you are completely satisfied.
